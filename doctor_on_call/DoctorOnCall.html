Sit in a circle. Each student gets a role. There is one doctor and one sick person. The doctor tries to find who is sick. The doctor has three chances to guess.
Doctor: "Do you have a _____?" Sick: "Yes, I do. I have a ______." Others: "No, I don't."
If the doctor guesses correctly, they get 2 points. If not, the other players get 1 point each.
