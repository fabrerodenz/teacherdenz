Sit in a circle. 👉 坐成一圈

Each student gets a role. 👉 每位學生都有一個角色

1 doctor, 1 sick person 👉 一位醫生，一位病人

The doctor finds who is sick. 👉 醫生要找出生病的人

The doctor has three chances to guess. 👉 醫生有三次機會猜

Doctor: "Do you have a _____?"

Sick: "Yes, I do. I have a ______."

Others: "No, I don't."

⭐ If the doctor guesses the sick person, the doctor gets 2 points.

⭐ If the doctor did not guess, the other students get 1 point each.
